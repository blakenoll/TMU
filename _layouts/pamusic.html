Carnegie Hall Performance with Maestro Paul Plew

You’re invited to join Dr. Paul Plew and The Master's University Chorale for a special performance of Dan Forrest’s Requiem for the Living at New York City’s famous Carnegie Hall. The concert will be held on Sunday, May 13, 2018, at 2:00pm, and includes a post-concert dinner cruise. Singers who wish to join will be sent the music in advance of the concert and are asked to participate in all rehearsals, May 11-13.

Both packages listed below do not include airfare or meals (except for the post-concert dinner cruise).

Package #1 - Performance only

$849 per person

includes performance fees, orchestra fees, and rental of Carnegie Hall for each rehearsal and performance, as well as the post-concert cruise. You will be responsible for your airfare, hotel, transportation and all meals.

Package #2 - Hotel accommodations and Performance

$1349 per person (4 people per hotel room)

$1654 per person (3 people per hotel room)

$1958 per person (2 people per hotel room)

$2479 for a single room

includes performance fees, orchestra fees, and rental of Carnegie Hall for each rehearsal and performance. This package also includes hotel accommodations at the Grand Hyatt Hotel at 42nd Street/Park Avenue for 4 nights/5 days, concert-related bus transportation as well as the post-concert dinner cruise.

Non-refundable Deposit - $300.00 due by October 15, 2017 for all packages.

Please send checks to The Master's University Alumni Office or call with a credit card (661.362.2203). Checks must be received in the office by October 15th. Payment schedule will be provided upon deposit.

For more details call Shayna Anderson, Alumni Office at 661-362-2203 (sanderson@masters.edu) or Pamela Plew, School of Music at 661-362-2256 pjplew@masters.edu. Payment schedule will be provided upon deposit.

Timeline of Events

  • Thursday - arrive in NYC
  • Friday - rehearsal (could be morning or afternoon, but evening is free)
  • Saturday - rehearsal (opposite timeframe from day before, but evening is still free)
  • Sunday - dress rehearsal, 2pm concert, post-concert cruise (depending on selected package, dinner on cruise may or may not be included)
  • Monday - departure
