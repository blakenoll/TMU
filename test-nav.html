When Saturday, September 30th, 2017

Location: Central Park, 27150 Bouquet Canyon Rd, Santa Clarita, CA 91350

COLLEGIATE ENTRY FEE*: $200 per gender or $350 per institution; $25 per individual/unattached.

HIGH SCHOOL ENTRY FEE*: $100 per race ($25 per individual for incomplete teams), with a $350 max per school. * Checks payable to The Master’s University. Cash or check may be brought to the meet or mailed to: C/O Zach Schroeder, The Master’s University, 21726 Placerita Canyon Road, Santa Clarita, CA 91321.

ENTRY DEADLINE: Monday, September 25th @ 11:00 PM pacific time (no race-day registration)

ENTRY PROCESS: All entries must be made through www.directathletics.com by the published deadline.

AWARDS: Top 10 individuals of each race will be presented with top finisher t-shirts. The winning team from each race will be presented with a trophy/plaque.

5K COURSE: 2 loop course that is approximately 85% dirt paths, 15% grass, 60% rolling terrain, and 40% flat. There is one small hill that will be run twice.

8K COURSE: 3 loop course that is approximately 85% dirt paths, 15% grass, 60% rolling terrain, and 40% flat. There is one small hill that will be run three times.

RACE SCHEDULE:

  • 7:45 am Race 1 – Men’s Collegiate 8K
  • 8:30 am Race 2 – Women’s Collegiate 5K
  • 9:00 am Race 3 – Boy's Varsity 5K
  • 9:30 am Race 4 – Girl's Varsity 5K
  • 10:00 am Race 5 – Boy's Junior Varsity 5K
  • 10:30 am Race 6 – Girl's Junior Varsity 5K

MEDICAL SAFETY GATE (high school only): To help ensure the safety of all runners, there will be a medical safety gate at the 1.75 mile mark. Any athlete that does not pass through the gate in less than 17 minutes must exit the course and will not be permitted to finish the race.

CHIP TIMING: Your team packet will include chips numbered and assigned to each athlete on your roster. Chips will be collected in the finish corral. If an athlete leaves the corral without returning their chip or if you have unused chips, they need to be returned to the finish corral or timing tent before you leave the meet. Teams will be charged $10 per missing chip.

FREE T-SHIRTS: Meet t-shirts will be handed out for free at our information booth to any high school athlete that provides a valid email address.

RESULTS AND SCORING: Results will be available at www.masters.edu/xcinvitational
